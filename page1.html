Q Tip Blooming flowers

This image shows the step by step of how to make these flowers. Because it rquires many q tips it will require more time to finish.

STEPS

  • Get Q Tips and cut a few in half. Half styrofoam balls. Stick the q tips all over the styrofoam ball.eave a space with no q tips on it for the stem Get a plastic plate and add paint to it.
  • Put the q tips that are stuck to the ball in the paint so they get colored. You can have different colors. Let dry 30 minutes.
  • Grab a stick or tree branch from outside and stick it in the space left of the ball.
